SORting Based on Educational Technology (SOR.B.E.T) Web App

About SORBET

SORBET idea is based on a large-setting kinectic game designed in the Polymechanon project . The SORBET online game authoring system was concieved, designed and developed by prof Chronis Kyigos, director of Educational Technology Lab, and Marianthi Grizioti (post doc researcher - developer). Parts of the applcilation were developed by the student E. Giama for her MA Thesis (2020).
